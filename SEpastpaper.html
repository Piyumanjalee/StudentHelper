1.a) Briefly describe how software being a Bespoke Software will affect the software cost estimation

Bespoke software, being custom-built to meet specific client needs, significantly impacts cost estimation
as it typically requires more time, resources, and specialized skills compared to off-the-shelf solutions. The unique and complex
requirements demand extensive analysis, frequent client interactions, and iterative development, which increase costs and make
estimation less predictable. Additionally, unforeseen challenges in implementing tailored features can further drive up expenses,
making bespoke software generally more expensive and harder to estimate accurately.
